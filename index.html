Healty - Mobile Skincare App

Type

UI UX Casestudy

Date

27.08.2024

Role

UI/UX designer

Duration

8 Weeks

Healty is an innovative mobile application designed to assist users in identifying and diagnosing various skin conditions through advanced machine learning and image processing techniques. By analyzing uploaded images of skin lesions, Healty provides users with potential diagnoses and facilitates access to qualified dermatologists for further consultation.

The challenge

Many individuals face challenges in identifying skin conditions accurately and promptly. Traditional methods for skin disease diagnosis often involve long wait times and require in-person visits to healthcare professionals. Healty aims to address these issues by offering a convenient, accessible, and early diagnostic tool for skin health.

My solution

Healty offers a mobile platform where users can upload images of their skin lesions. The app uses a machine learning model to analyze the images and provide potential diagnoses. Users can then receive detailed information about the detected conditions and consult with dermatologists directly through the app.

Florasight

Type

Casestudy

Date

27.05.2024

Role

UI UX designer

Duration

7 Weeks

Florasight is an automated plant disease detector website that leverages machine learning techniques to detect and diagnose plant diseases. The website aims to provide a user-friendly interface where users can upload images of their plants, and the system will analyze them to identify any potential diseases or issues.

The challenge

Gardeners and farmers struggle to identify plant diseases accurately, leading to delayed treatment and reduced yields. Existing detection solutions are complex and require specialized knowledge, limiting accessibility. This complexity hampers timely diagnosis, impacting plant health and agricultural productivity.

My solution

I propose that Florasight will have a streamlined interface enabling gardeners and farmers to easily upload plant images for quick diagnosis using drag-and-drop functionality that supports multiple uploads. Machine learning algorithms will deliver instant diagnoses with clear insights and treatment suggestions. The platform will also include educational resources like articles, videos, and a community forum for sharing experiences. Collaboration with agricultural experts will enhance diagnosis accuracy and facilitate user consultations. Throughout the design process, I will conduct user research, create wireframes, and perform usability testing to ensure an optimal experience across devices.

Corpit International Landing Page

Type

UI UX Design

Date

13.09.2024

Role

UI UX Designer

Duration

2 Weeks

Corpit International is dedicated to enhancing patient care through precision-engineered devices for orthopedics and gastroenterology. As a UI/UX designer, I was tasked with creating a website that reflects our commitment to innovation and effectively communicates our product offerings to healthcare professionals.

The challenge

The challenge was to design a website from scratch that would effectively serve healthcare professionals seeking reliable medical devices. It needed to convey trust and professionalism while providing an intuitive user experience.

My solution

I conducted user research to understand healthcare professionals' needs, leading to a user-centered design with intuitive navigation and a modern, professional look. The responsive layout ensures seamless access across devices, while a dedicated product showcase features detailed descriptions and high-quality images. By implementing accessibility best practices, the redesigned website successfully enhances user experience and effectively communicates Corpit International's values.
